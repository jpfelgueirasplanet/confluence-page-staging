m-hospitality - Product Specification

General information

The integration to this Guest Relation Management vendor is done for protel Air and protel onPrem and is presently one-way only. 

m-hospitality’s state of the art GRM uses patented algorithms and machine-learning technology to identify and segment personas within your data.
This presents an unrivalled opportunity to cross-sell services to guests, to make their experience even more of a pleasure and to target specific segments with tailored offers.
It will also encourage guests to spend more money during their stay.  m-hospitality provides cutting-edge mobile and web technology solutions that can be summarized in three main products; mobile applications, guest relationship management, and loyalty. With more than 10 awards in less than three years, m-hospitality’s guest engagement platform has proven to increase guests’ satisfaction, while cross-selling hotels’ outlets and increasing incremental revenues. The company’s vision is to change the future of hospitality by providing user-friendly solutions that let premium hotels and resorts unleash their full potential, while providing guests with unique experiences.

Reservations as well as stay information after the check out of the guest are sent from protel to m-hospitality.
