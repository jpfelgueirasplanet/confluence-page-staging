Guiding questions for Support Tickets

Please make sure the ticket is remaining all relavent details in order to check your issue:

  • Is only one invoice affected? If so, which one? 
  • Are all invoices affected?
  • Have you checked for a CorrelationID in Kibana?
  • Is the problem reproducible on test environment?

Since the fiscal was broken while trying to fiscalized the invoices which are missing the QR code on it, there is no way to retrigger the reprint the QR code. In addition, the invoice templates do not have any issue here.


 The correct and only way to solve this kind of issue is to do the following:

  • Void & Repost the invoice
  • Fiscalize the invoice

In addition, manual api trigger to send out the IO_InvoiceFiscalizeRQ will not help here.
