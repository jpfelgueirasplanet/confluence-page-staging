General information

The two - way integration to this CRM vendor is done for onprem product
The NumBirds CRM is Marketing Automation Omnichannel where travel agencies, hotel, tourist information or tour operators can analyze their travel data and customer data. They send individualized marketing campaigns to all their customers including  transactional campaigns.
