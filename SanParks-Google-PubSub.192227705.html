General information

The integration to this vendor is done for Protel Cloud PMS (pAir)
Pub/Sub is a publish/subscribe (Pub/Sub) service, a messaging service where the senders of messages are decoupled from the receivers of messages.
The primary function of utilising the Google Pub/Sub is to act as the Central layer across multiple SanParks integration systems
