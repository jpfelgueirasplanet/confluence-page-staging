HTNG_WakeupSchedulingNotifRQ/RS

Message Description

                                                                             

  • Wake Up Call Scheduling is used to create, modify, or delete a wakeup call.  The receiver is obligated to fulfill this request
  • A new, modified, or deleted wakeup call is entered into Wakeup Notification Producer system and sends out notifications to Wakeup Notification Consumers regarding wakeup calls (Wakeup call creation, Wakeup call deletion, Wakeup call completion)

Preconditions

  • A subscription to the wakeup calls

Postconditions

  • None
