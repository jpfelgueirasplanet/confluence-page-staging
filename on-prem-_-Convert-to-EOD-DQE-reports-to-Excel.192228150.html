General Information

The purpose of this ckit is to create two export files in XLS format of the following Hotel Status data during the EOD into the protel "Export" folder


  • With Allotment data  (K00553_io_allot.rpt)
  • Without Allotment data (K00553_io_ohneallot.rpt)
