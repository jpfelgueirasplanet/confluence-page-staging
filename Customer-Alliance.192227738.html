Product Specification - Customer Alliance


General information

The I/O integration of this online reputation and customer experience management vendor is done for the pAir and on-premise products.

The integration in one-way from protel and the data sent to Customer Alliance from Protel I/O is used for multiple purposes.

Firstly it is displayed in a guest list within the customer alliance platform, where customers may view the status of messages being sent to that guest.

Secondly, it is used to contact guests in order to communicate messages or deliver invitations to leave reviews.

Lastly, it may be used to calculate statistics in the customer alliance  platform; for example, when trying to view statistics grouped by age group or customer type, we may use data from Protel I/O to classify guests.
