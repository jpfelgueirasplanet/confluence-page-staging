One way Installation


1

Create a test reservation in the future (at least one year after the system date & provide the reservation number to the integration partner

  • make a modification and finally a cancellation of your test reservation. 
  • always provide the reservation number to your integration partner, so that the reservation can afterwards be cancelled

Check if Integration Partner recieved the reservation

e.G: check:

  • Arrival date 
  • Departure Date
  • Guest Name

Verify in Kibana that:

  • outgoing OTA_HotelResNotifRQ is answered by OTA_HotelResNotifRS and no error occurs

  • outgoing OTA_HotelInvCountNotifRQ is answered by OTA_HotelInvCountRS and no error occurs

2

Create and start bulk uploads

Integration Partner needs at least 1 year of historic Data to build statistic

  • OTA_HotelResNotifRQ
    • for the past 1 year and ending with 1 year after last reservation OTB (rounded up)
  • OTA_HoteInvCountNotifRQ
    • for the past 1 year and ending with 1 year after last reservation OTB (rounded up)


BTS Jobs will be processed one by one as only one bulk per property is allowed at the same time. 

Bulks should be starting with 1 January and ending with 31 of December (i.e. 01.01.2017 - 31.12.2021).

In case you have issues with a stuck bulk upload, investigate why messages were not sent. As an option, delete the old job and create a new one. 

3

Data Validation Report - DQE

Create DQE Report according to the Bulk Upload Date Range and send to integration Partner 

  • after checking if the bulk successfully finished, provide the integration partner the report
  • The Integration partner will validate the DQE 
  • The report is to be found in the tab Office (Büro) of the front office in pAir.
  • To verify if all messages send via Bulk confirm total number with Integration Partner 
  • SCS Job Details e.G. OTA_HotelInvCountNotifRQ
    • Start date: 2023-06-26 10:52
      End date: 2023-06-26 10:52
    • Messages Total:  49
      Messages Processed: 49
      Messages Failed: 0
  • Kibana Log for the send message and Date/Time range 



4

Update the protel SMP page with the information about the installed customer
