Traveldata - Product Specification

General information

The integration to this CRS vendor is completed both ways for OnPremise only. The one-way will send availabilities, rate plans and restrictions, whereas the 2-way will allow the integration partner to update reservations and query a list of reservations for a specific day using the OTA_ReadRQ message.

OTA_HotelResNotifRQ

 

Reservation details, creations, modifications and cancellations 

 

OTA_HotelRatePlanNotifRQ

 
  • Up to 4 children rates are supported by TravelData (usual standard age range is 0-5 and 6-12)
  • Packages in the rates are not supported by TravelData
  • Virtual Rates are supported

 

OTA_HotelAvailNotifRQ

 

Restriction values on Room Type, Property Level, per date. 

Traveldata supports the following restrictions:
- Close Out
- Close to Arrival
- Minimum Length of Stay by stay
- Minimum Length of Stay by arrival

 

OTA_HotelInvCountNotifRQ

 

Available rooms per room type 

 

OTA_ReadRQ

 

Reservation lookup
