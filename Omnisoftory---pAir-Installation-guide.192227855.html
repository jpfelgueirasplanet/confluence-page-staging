About the Interface:  omnisoftory

In Switzerland, accommodations have to perform several administrative tasks related to the personal data of their guests. They have to submit reports to legal authorities such as the Federal Statistical Office (HESTA), the cantonal police, the tourist tax collection centre, the Tourism Observatory and the issuing of guest cards. Customer data is collected several times, but never in the same way or for the same entities. Easycheckin, a product powered by Omnisftory is a web application for touristic area, which can simplify customer data collection in accommodation and automate distribution to the organizations concerned.

With Easycheckin service facilitate this taking of information from the customer and transmission to each party concerned with an online IT platform. It will bring together all the data regarding the identity of the customer as well as a lot of information about the travel behavior of the host. In destinations/areas where Easycheckin has been deployed, you can login to your accommodation account provided by the region. With Protel > Easycheckin interface, reservation data are sync into your accommodation account.

One way Installation


1

Create a test reservation in the future (at least one year after the system date) & provide the reservation number to your integration partner

  • make a modification and finally a cancellation of your test reservation. Verify in Kibana that the outgoing OTA_HotelResNotifRQ is answered by OTA_HotelResNotifRS and no error occurs. 

Check the next day (after an EOD)  the OTA_HotelStayInfoNotifRQ messages in Kibana, verify in Kibana that the OTA_HotelStayInfoNotifRQ is answered by the OTA_HotelStayInfoNotifRS and no errors are present


2

Create and start bulk uploads

  • for reservations (OTA_HotelResNotifRQ)
    for the last 2 years and ending on 31st December 2099.

  • for revenue attached to profiles (OTA_HotelStayInfoNotifRQ) 
    for the last 2 years and to current date.



BTS Jobs will be processed one by one as only one bulk per property is allowed at the same time. 

Bulks should be starting with 1 January and ending with 31 of December (i.e. 01.01.2021 - 31.12.2021).

In case you have issues with a stuck bulk upload, investigate why messages were not sent. As an option, delete the old job and create a new one.
