    One way Installation


    1

    Create a test reservation in the future (at least one year after the system date) & provide the reservation number to your integration partner

    • make a modification and finally a cancellation of your test reservation. Verify in Kibana that outgoing OTA_HotelResNotifRQ is answered by OTA_HotelResNotifRS and no error occurs. 


    2

    Test RoomStatus update outbound
    (HTNG_HotelRoomStatusUpdateNotifRQ)

    • Modify the room status of a random room to the different statuses
      Check the HTNG_HotelRoomStatusUpdateNotifRS sent by the partner 

    Partner support the following RoomStatus:

    • CLEAN
    • DIRTY
    • OUT OF SERVICE
    • TOUCHED
    • CLEANING IN PROGRESS
    • CHECKED
    • OUT OF ORDER

    3

    Create and start bulk uploads

    • for reservations (OTA_HotelResNotifRQ)
      for  one week in the past and ending with one week in the future


    BTS Jobs will be processed one by one as only one bulk per property is allowed at the same time. 

    Bulks should be starting with 1 January and ending with 31 of December (i.e. 01.01.2017 - 31.12.2021).

    In case you have issues with a stuck bulk upload, investigate why messages were not sent. As an option, delete the old job and create a new one. 


    4

    Data Validation Report - DQE

    No DQE required - compare the number of messages sent for  bulk upload


     

    Two way Installation

    Examples

    1

    Test incoming RoomStatus update 
    (HTNG_HotelRoomStatusUpdateNotifRQ)

    • provide a RoomID 
    • let the partner modify the Room Status to random room statuses
    • check the Room Status of the reservation in the PMS 


    Partner support the following RoomStatus:

    • CLEAN
    • DIRTY
    • OUT OF SERVICE
    • TOUCHED
    • CLEANING IN PROGRESS
    • CHECKED


    Sample Message HTNG_HotelRoomStatusUpdateNotifRQ
    <ns2:HTNG_HotelRoomStatusUpdateNotifRQ
    xmlns:ns2="http://htng.org/2014B" CorrelationID="#36323838-3631-4635-b033-353039393433#realm_3750">
    <ns2:POS>
    <ns1:Source
    xmlns:ns1="http://www.opentravel.org/OTA/2003/05">
    <ns1:RequestorID Type="10" ID_Context="protelIO" ID="2989"/>
    </ns1:Source>
    </ns2:POS>
    <ns2:PropertyInfo HotelCode="2989" HotelCodeContext="protelIO"/>
    <ns2:Room RoomID="305">
    <ns2:RoomType IsRoom="true" RoomTypeCode="Komfort DZ" RoomID="305"/>
    <ns2:HKStatus>NEEDS_INSPECTION</ns2:HKStatus>
    </ns2:Room>
    </ns2:HTNG_HotelRoomStatusUpdateNotifRQ>

    2

    Test an incoming posting
    (OTA_ReadRQ) 

    Request a specific reservation ID (protel internal ID)


    Sample message OTA_ReadRQ
    <?xml version='1.0' encoding='utf-8'?>
    <OTA_ReadRQ xmlns="http://www.opentravel.org/OTA/2003/05" CorrelationID="UseCase1" TimeStamp="2021-08-26T10:10:00Z">
    <POS>
    <Source>
    <RequestorID ID="3152" Type="10" ID_Context="protelIO"/>
    </Source>
    </POS>
    <ReadRequests>
    <ReadRequest>
    <UniqueID ID="345465" ID_Context="protelIO" Type="14"/>
    </ReadRequest>
    </ReadRequests>
    </OTA_ReadRQ>
