Product Specification

General information

Revenue Insight is a commercial hotel business intelligence platform built on PMS data, helping users to make better business decisions.

It combines a wide range of indicators (such as rate codes, channels, room types, travel agents etc.) for users to track their Revenue Management performance and find revenue drivers and opportunities. 

The main functionalities of the product include:

  • KPI performance monitoring for past & future dates, vs time references or targets
  • Stay date analysis
  • Cancellation analysis
  • Pick-up and Pace visualisations
  • Drill-down dynamic analysis options with cross-selections and multiple filters.
