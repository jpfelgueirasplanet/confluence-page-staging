General information

The integration to this CRM vendor is done for pAir NG product
KunLeiSys is a software that works together with your existing hotel program and extends it with some functions. The core of our KunLeiSys software is the guest club, where all tasks and possibilities are managed. The guest club is easily integrated into your existing homepage and thus offers your guests numerous new possibilities.Guests collect loyalty points with and during every stay. Guests receive loyalty points not only in the form of x% of the invoice total or the days of stay as a credit, but also for each recommendation, for each hotel rating or even annually as a small birthday gift. All loyalty points values are individually adjustable. The guests can then manage the collected loyalty points in the guest club on your homepage. Each guest automatically receives his own access data and his own page where he can view and redeem his loyalty points.Collected points can either be converted directly into a hotel, massage or value voucher and redeemed, given away to friends/acquaintances or redeemed for a discount when reserving a package offered exclusively to guest club members.Guests can invite friends or acquaintances to your guest club and will receive the deposited reward after the friend or acquaintance has completed their stay.
