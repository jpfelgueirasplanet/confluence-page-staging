Product Specification



General information

The integration to this vendor is done for Concierge Organiser (CO). Concierge Organizer is a staff facing app, assisting the concierge with information about the guest reservation and guest profile.  The concierge can book services for the guest and send this information via a trace to protel.  Once the service has been consumed by the guest, the posting of the revenue can be sent from Concierge Organizer to protel.

on prem

pAir

OTA_HotelResNotifRQ

New reservations, modifications and cancellations are sent from protel to Concierge Organizer (CO).

OTA_ReadRQ / OTA_ResRetrieveRS

Reservations can be looked up by CO from protel

OTA_ProfileCreateRQ

Newly created profiles are sent from protel to CO.  Please note, CO only considers profiles which are already attached to reservations.

OTA_ProfileModifyRQ

Profile modifications are sent from protel to CO. 

OTA_ReadRQ / OTA_ProfileReadRS

CO can read profiles from protel.

IO_ResModifyNotifRQ

CO adds and modifies traces in protel.  The information of booked services are sent in this way from CO to protel.  In case a service is cancelled, the trace is NOT deleted, CO will modify the trace and add an information that the service has been cancelled.

HTNG_HotelFolioRQ

CO can call up the invoices of the guest.  This is used to show the concierge the different invoices which could be used for posting.  The concierge can pick one of the invoices and send the posting to the guest invoice.

HTNG_ChargePostingRQ

CO can post revenue and correction (negative revenue) to the invoices of the guest.

IO_SystemDataRQ

CO can call up the trace departments and articles/transaction accounts directly from the protel system data.
