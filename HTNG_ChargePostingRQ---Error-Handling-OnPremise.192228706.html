ShortText="Transaction error - please report. Incomplete Posting flow. Please check the guest folio before a retry

If no RS is generated by the PMS service within ~55 seconds, then:
  • the ESB will not do retries itself
  • the ESB will generate a generic RS to inform the sender of the RQ about the failure.

! The RQ should not be automatically retried - the posting itself could have happened, yet for some reason - no RS could have been generated by the PMS service (e.g. fiscalization error). IO-7390
