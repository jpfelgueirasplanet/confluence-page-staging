SONAS walk-in functionality

General information

This document outlines the re-certification process for the SONAS integration, specifically focusing on the walk-in functionality. SONAS is a Kiosk System that offers bidirectional capabilities for check-in and check-out operations, posting charges to the Property Management System (PMS) invoices, and modifying guest profiles. The SONAS Kiosk System enables hotel guests to perform the following tasks without the need to visit the reception desk:

  1. Check-In and Check-Out Operations
    • Guests can search for their reservation using the reservation number.
    • They can complete the check-in and check-out processes directly at the kiosk.
  2. Payment Processing
    • Guests can view and pay any outstanding balances on their invoices.
  3. Invoice Details
    • Guests can access and review the details of their invoices.
  4. Profile Modification
    • Guests have the ability to modify their profile information.

The SONAS I/O Integration is certified for On-Premise deployments only. This certification process focuses on ensuring the seamless integration and functionality of the SONAS Kiosk System within an on-premises environment.

Testing Notes:

  1. The following tests included a trial check-in with a 'cleaned' room pre-assigned (Test 8), a 'checked' room pre-assigned (Test 10), and another without room assignment (Test 12). All three results were anticipated based on a shared understanding of how the system behaves.
  2. For walk-in guests, SONAS will create bookings without room assignment and allow protel to automatically assign 'Checked' rooms at check-in. 
  3. As a future development consideration, SONAS would like to exercise an API call that will remove a pre-assigned room if that room is not yet available when the guest comes to check-in at the kiosk. They wish to remove the allocated room and have protel auto-assign an available/'checked' room during the check-in process.
