One way Installation


1

Create a test reservation in the future (at least one year after the system date) & provide the reservation number to your integration partner

  • make a modification and finally a cancellation of your test reservation. Verify in Kibana that outgoing OTA_HotelResNotifRQ is answered by OTA_HotelResNotifRS and no error occurs. 


2

Test HTNG_HotelFolioNotifRQ

  • Add a charge to the Folio of the above reservation and verify in Kibana that HTNG_HotelFolioNotifRQ is answered by HTNG_HotelFolioNotifRS and no error occurs.


3

Test  OTA_ProfileCreateRQ/OTA_ProfileModifyRQ 

Experience Hotel is not saving a profile if it is not attached to a reservation. Thus, to test OTA_ProfileCreateRQ/OTA_ProfileModifyRQ 

  • Modify the profile of the test reservation created during the previous OTA_HotelResNotifRQ
  • Create test examples of the individual, company profiles. Verify in Kibana that outgoing OTA_ProfileCreateRQ is answered by RS and no error occurs.
  • Modify the individual company profiles by changing name and address details. Verify in Kibana that the OTA_ProfileModifyRQ is answered by the a OTA_ProfileModifyRS and no errors are present. 
  • Cancel your test reservation afterwards.


4

Create and start bulk uploads

  • for reservations (OTA_HotelResNotifRQ)
    for the last     2 years and ending with the next 5 years (please clarify with Experience Hotel and customer)

  • for revenue attached to reservations (OTA_HotelStayInfoNotifRQ) 
    for the last 2 years (please also clarify with Experience Hotel and customer)

BTS Jobs will be processed one by one as only one bulk per property is allowed at the same time. 

Bulks should be starting with 1 January and ending with 31 of December (i.e. 01.01.2017 - 31.12.2021).

In case you have issues with a stuck bulk upload, investigate why messages were not sent. As an option, delete the old job and create a new one. 


5

Data Validation Report - DQE

  • Check that the bulk is successfully finished 
  • The DQE is NOT mandatory, only in case of obvious discrepancies a check should be done together with the Experience Hotel Support team.

 

Two way Installation

Examples

1

Test read reservation 

(OTA_ReadRQ)

  • Create a test reservation in the future
  • ask the Integration partner to send a OTA_ReadRQ for this reservation in the future.
  • Verify in Kibana that the OTA_ReadRQ is answered by OTA_ResRetrieveRS and there are no errors


2

Test incoming profile

(OTA_ProfileModifyRQ)

  • the integration partner should make a change to the profile in the above reservation
  • Verify in Kibana that the OTA_ProfileModifyRQ is answered by OTA_ProfileModifyRS and that the changes made are reflected correctly in the protel profile and there are no errors



3

Update the developer portal with the information about the installed customer
