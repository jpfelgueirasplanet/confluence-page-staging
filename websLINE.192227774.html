Product Specification - websLINE, CRS

General information

https://www.websline.com

websLINE-ABM is a

  • booking system
  • request manager
  • messenger
  • deposit payment 
  • and more than 35 special modules specially tailored to the needs of the hotel industry

make the websLINE ABM V5 the Swiss Army Knife for hotels.


The websline-ABMv5 is a full-fledged direct booking system and at the same time manages 100% of all vacation inquiries with the integrated correspondence or inquiry manager.

The high-performance Web Booking Engine (IBE) in the ABM V5 is state-of-the-art and can be seamlessly integrated into your website.
Of course, all elements are fully responsive and the operation is optimally tailored to each end device.

A highlight is the permanent 2-way function, which enables the guest to inquire during the entire operating process or to book directly online.
This leads to a reduction in the bounce rate and an improvement in the booking rate.

Fully automated interfaces to your hotel program, channel manager, travel insurance or payment provider ensure smooth data exchange between the systems.

Integrated messenger for perfect communication.
Guests often have questions about your offers or their booked stay. Most of this communication takes place via email, and important data is often lost in the process.
With the integrated messenger, the ABM V5 offers a completely new kind of comprehensive guest communication! Every communication is saved in the system - nothing is lost!

You and your employees can operate all components with just one login - easy, time-saving, efficient!
All systems communicate 100% with each other and collect data for a consistent profiling of your guests.
