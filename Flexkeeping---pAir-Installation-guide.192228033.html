One way Installation


1

Create a test reservation for the current bussines date at the PMS & provide the reservation number to your integration partner

  • make a check in and then undo the check in  test reservation. Verify in Kibana that outgoing OTA_HotelResNotifRQ is answered by OTA_HotelResNotifRS and no error occurs. 
  • always provide the reservation number to your integration partner, so that the reservation can afterwards be cancelled 


2

Test RoomStatus update outbound
(HTNG_HotelRoomStatusUpdateNotifRQ)

  • Modify the room status of a random room to the different statuses
    Check the HTNG_HotelRoomStatusUpdateNotifRS sent by the partner 

Partner support the following RoomStatus:

  • CLEAN
  • DIRTY
  • OUT OF SERVICE
  • TOUCHED
  • CLEANING IN PROGRESS
  • CHECKED
  • OUT OF ORDER

3

Create and start bulk uploads

  • for reservations (OTA_HotelResNotifRQ)
    for  one week in the past and ending with one week in the future


BTS Jobs will be processed one by one as only one bulk per property is allowed at the same time. 

In case you have issues with a stuck bulk upload, investigate why messages were not sent. As an option, delete the old job and create a new one. 


4

Data Validation Report - DQE

No DQE required - compare the number of messages sent for  bulk upload


 

Two way Installation

Examples

1

Test an incoming posting
(HTNG_ChargePostingRQ)

  • provide the ReservationID of the test reservation
  • integ. partner posts an amount on the folio (i.e. 10 Euro)
  • open the folio in the PMS and check if the amount was posted correctly
  • integ.partner needs to send charge with negative amount to void it 
Sample message HTNG_ChargePostingRQ
<HTNG_ChargePostingRQ
xmlns="http://htng.org/2014B" EchoToken="EchoToken_String" Version="0" CorrelationID="CID_string24">
<POS>
<Source
xmlns="http://www.opentravel.org/OTA/2003/05">
<RequestorID ID="3137" Type="10" ID_Context="protelIO"/>
<BookingChannel Type="4" Primary="true"/>
</Source>
</POS>
<Posting ID="1">
<Transaction TicketID="1">
<RevenueDetails>
<RevenueDetail Description="Breakfast" PMSRevenueCode="83" CurrencyCode="EUR" Amount="12.00" SubTypeID="Revenue">
<ExtendedPrice Quantity="1"/>
<Account ID="80" Type="14" ID_Context="protelIO"/>
</RevenueDetail>
</RevenueDetails>
</Transaction>
</Posting>
</HTNG_ChargePostingRQ>

2

Test incoming RoomStatus update 
(HTNG_HotelRoomStatusUpdateNotifRQ)

  • provide a RoomID 
  • let the partner modify the Room Status to random room statuses
  • check the Room Status of the reservation in the PMS 


Partner support the following RoomStatus:

  • CLEAN
  • DIRTY
  • OUT OF SERVICE
  • TOUCHED
  • CLEANING IN PROGRESS
  • CHECKED


Sample Message HTNG_HotelRoomStatusUpdateNotifRQ
<ns2:HTNG_HotelRoomStatusUpdateNotifRQ
xmlns:ns2="http://htng.org/2014B" CorrelationID="#36323838-3631-4635-b033-353039393433#realm_3750">
<ns2:POS>
<ns1:Source
xmlns:ns1="http://www.opentravel.org/OTA/2003/05">
<ns1:RequestorID Type="10" ID_Context="protelIO" ID="2989"/>
</ns1:Source>
</ns2:POS>
<ns2:PropertyInfo HotelCode="2989" HotelCodeContext="protelIO"/>
<ns2:Room RoomID="305">
<ns2:RoomType IsRoom="true" RoomTypeCode="Komfort DZ" RoomID="305"/>
<ns2:HKStatus>NEEDS_INSPECTION</ns2:HKStatus>
</ns2:Room>
</ns2:HTNG_HotelRoomStatusUpdateNotifRQ>
