General information

What is the Global Hotel Alliance?

Global Hotel Alliance (GHA) was founded in 2004, and is based on the airline alliance model, which claims to be the world’s largest alliance of independent hotel brands.

The Global Hotel Alliance is a fusion of more than 30 international hotel brands with more than 550 hotels in 78 countries. Every single hotel not only impresses with its luxurious interior, but also with its amazing location and incorporation of local cultures and traditions.

What is GHA DISCOVERY?

GHA DISCOVERY is the multi-brand loyalty program from GHA, claiming to have over 5 million members. GHA DISCOVERY rewards its members with hotel benefits and local experiences to make the experience in the hotels extra special.
