Swayed - Product Specification

General information

Swayed is an Upsell System with a 2-way functionality for posting amounts on a folio and to receive the reservation status information about cancellations, modifications, CheckIns and CheckOuts in order to display these infos to the guest by application. The guest can use the application to realize discounts on special offered items (i.e. Coca Cola with discount) and he can see the current status of his reservation.

Swayed I/O Integration is certified for On-Premise.
