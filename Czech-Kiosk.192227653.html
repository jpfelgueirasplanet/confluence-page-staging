Product Specification - Czech Kiosk

General information

CzechKiosk is a Kiosk System with a 2-way functionality for Checking In/Out reservations, posting amounts on PMS invoices and doing modifications for profiles.

The guest can perform a Check In/Out at the Kiosk by searching his booking via reservation number, paying an open invoice balance and also checking invoice details, without visiting the reception desk.

CzechKiosk I/O Integration is certified for onPrem so far.
