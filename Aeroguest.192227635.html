Aeroguest - Product Specification

General information

Aeroguest is a Kiosk System with a 2-way functionality for Checking In/Out reservations, posting amounts and articles on PMS invoices and do modifications for profiles. 

The guest can perform a Check In/Out at the Kiosk by searching his booking via reservation number, paying an open invoice balance or posting articles and also checking invoice details, without visiting the reception desk.

Aeroguest I/O Integration is certified for onPrem so far.

WalkIn functionality is not yet supported by Aeroguest
