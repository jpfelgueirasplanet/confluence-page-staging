Amadeus GMS - Product Specification

General information

Amadeus GMS (Formerly Travelclick GMS) is a CRM system that supports the receipt of reservations, profile, allotments and guest stay information as part of the one way interface from protel.
The 2-way for Amadeus GMS is able to modify guest profiles in protel, including guest and room preferences
