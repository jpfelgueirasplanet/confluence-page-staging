OTA_ReadRQ | inbound | Wildcard Search

PWS-2427

IO

ChargePosting | inbound | different behaviour for payment and charges

For posted payments or revenue to a reservation ID the RS will hold a warning element if the payment / revenue was posted to a different folio window. If there are already folio windows closed for this reservation, the PWS will try to post the payment/revenue to the next free folio window. The RS will hold the used folio window in the warning element.
