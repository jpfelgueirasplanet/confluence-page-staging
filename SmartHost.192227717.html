SmartHost - Product Specification

General information

The integration to this CRM vendor is done for both PMS products. Versions 1 & 2 are currently retired and only V3 is available for new customers.

Smart Host is a comprehensive CRM and guest marketing platform designed specifically for hotels. It leverages your PMS data to generate intelligent guest profiles, enabling the delivery of AI-driven automated campaigns such as loyalty programs and reservation-related communication (Pre-, Post-, and Instay emails). With Smart Host, you have complete control over your guest communication throughout the entire lifecycle, using a single tool that ensures timely, personalized messages delivered through the most appropriate channels.
