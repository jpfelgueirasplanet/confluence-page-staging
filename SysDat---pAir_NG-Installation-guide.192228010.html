One way Installation


1

Create a test reservation in the future (at least one year after the system date) and provide the reservation number to your integration partner

  • make a modification of your test reservation. Verify in Kibana that outgoing OTA_HotelResNotifRQ is answered by OTA_HotelResNotifRS and no error occurs
  • Verify in Kibana that the outgoing messages OTA_HotelInvCountNotifRQ is answered with RS message from the Integration system


2

Test outbound allotment creation

(OTA_HotelInvBlockNotifRQ)

Create a test allotment and include the test reservation and provide the allotment name to your integration partner

Verify in Kibana that outgoing OTA_HotelInvBlockNotifRQ is answered by OTA_HotelInvBlockNotifRS and no error occurs


3

Cancel the test reservation and the test allotment


4

Create and start bulk upload

  • for reservations (OTA_HotelResNotifRQ) in the past 1 year, and next 2 years from the installation date
  • for allotments (OTA_HotelInvBlockNotifRQ) in the past 1 year, and next 2 years from the installation date
  • for folios (HTNG_HotelFolioNotifRQ) for the past 2 and all future from the installation date (please set 2099 in the future)

BTS Jobs will be processed one by one as only one bulk per property is allowed at the same time. 

Bulks should be starting with 1 January and ending with 31 of December (i.e. 01.01.2017 - 31.12.2021).

In case you have issues with a stuck bulk upload, investigate why messages were not sent. As an option, delete the old job and create a new one.
