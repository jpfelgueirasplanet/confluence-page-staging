Large InvCount Message during the EOD

pAir triggers during each EOD all Reservtion, that are modified during the day, and the InvCountNotif for the next 400 days. 

The number of days that are sent out during the EOD, can be configured in BOB. But this number will be used for all integrations that are using the InvCountNotif.

Open the pAir Installation for the specific property

  • IO.Gateway.OTA_HotelInvCountNotifRQ.StatsDaysOffSet
