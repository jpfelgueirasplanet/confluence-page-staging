ReGuest - Product specifications


General information

ReGuest is the digital room seller that incorporates a sales software which recognises and exploits the revenue potential of the hotel's rooms and additional services in every phase of the guest journey.

The CRM brings a whole array of advantages. It helps improve the Guest Experience, maximize the turnover, and strengthen guest loyalty. It automatizes many processes, provides with detailed analyses and thereby creates the base for targeted marketing campaigns. Those could be automated sales with Auto Selection or the mailing of personalized newsletters with Sales Campaigns.

ReGuest CRM system creates guest profiles based on various criteria. These include things like age and place of origin, just like guest status (new or regular), holiday type (family, couple, sports etc.), dietary habits and interests. It saves their past stays, inquiry source, turnover generated and their purchasing power in their profile.

With ReGuest, the customers can maximise the sale of additional services thanks to the upselling function, send profiled newsletters that strengthen guest loyalty and chat with your guests in Messenger and on WhatsApp.

ReGuest I/O Integration is certified for pAir/NG and without a pilot for protel On Premise.
