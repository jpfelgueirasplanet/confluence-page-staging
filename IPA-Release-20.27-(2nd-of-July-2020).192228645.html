IPA-611

Outbound | Stats | Number of days in messages is higher than requested

Fix calculating the number of days back to be shown in OTA_HotelStatsNotifRQ. Previously it was one day more.

IPA-612

Inbound | Reservation | Accept <RoomRate> without date information

Accept reservation without "RoomRate/@EffectiveDate and RoomRate/@ExpiryDate" in RoomRate element.
Please note in this case the "@EffectiveDate and @ExpiryDate" should be present in TimeSpan element.
