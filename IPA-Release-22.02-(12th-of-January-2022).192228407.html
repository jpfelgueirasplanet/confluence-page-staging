Reservation | Outbound | TPA_Extension | Send Fixed Charges w/ ServiceDates

Add the new Element "SerivceDates"

This Element will be displayed for the Recurrence= "floating"

Possible Recurrence

  • OnArrival
  • OnDeparture
  • Daily
  • DaysOfWeek
  • Floating
  • Fixed
