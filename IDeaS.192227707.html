IDeaS - Product Specification

General information

The integration to the RMS system from IdeaS is done for 2 different products (G2 and G3).
Both are full Revenue Management Systems with a 2-way functionality.
G3 has more functionalities on RMS system side e.g. sending BAR (Best available rates) by LOS (Length of stay) on room type level instead of property level.

Special developments

Filters

There are several Filters existing on ESB level in order to change specific values within the outbound messages.
This has been done to customize the standard OTA messages according to IDeaS requirements.
Without those changes, IDeaS can't process the messages properly on their end.

IO-3015

Reservations with ResStatus "Requested"(OOA)/"Waitlisted" are unknown for IDeaS and can't be processed.

Solution for new bookings:
The message gets filtered out on ESB level, a RS get sent back by the ESB with a Warning:

<OTA_HotelResNotifRS xmlns="http://www.opentravel.org/OTA/2003/05">
<Warnings>
<Warning>367 - Invalid Format: @ResStatus="Requested" or @ResStatus="Waitlisted" is rejected/not processed by the system</Warning>
</Warnings>
</OTA_HotelResNotifRS>

Solution for modifications of existing bookings:
The values get changed, because they are not interested in reservations which are not deducting the availability.

OTA_HotelResNotifRQ / HotelReservations / HotelReservation @ResStatus
→ gets changed from "Requested" to "Cancelled" / "Waitlisted" to "Cancelled"

OTA_HotelResNotifRQ @ResStatus
→ gets changed from "Modify" to "Cancel"

OTA_HotelRatePlanNotifRQ

OTA_HotelRatePlanNotifRQ / RatePlans / RatePlan / Rates / Rate / AdditionalGuestAmounts / AdditionalGuestAmount

e.g. if IDeaS sends us an AdditionalGuestAmount for adults of 30 €, we would add BaseByGuestAmt lines up to 6 adults adding always 30 € to the previous line.
